About Jeeva Trust

Jeeva Trust is dedicated to empowering communities through Self-Help Groups (SHGs). Our mission is to provide financial inclusion, community support, and sustainable development opportunities.

Our Mission:
  • Empower communities through collaborative efforts
  • Provide access to financial services
  • Support sustainable development initiatives
  • Build strong, resilient communities
Our Vision:

To create a world where every community has access to the resources and support they need to thrive and grow.
